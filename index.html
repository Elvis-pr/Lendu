Welcome to LendU

A modern peer-to-peer lending platform for university students in Rwanda. We empower students to responsibly lend and borrow small loans within their university community with ease and security.

About LendU

LendU is a student-focused peer-to-peer lending platform designed to connect borrowers and lenders within university communities across Rwanda. Our platform offers secure loan management, transparent AI-driven risk assessment, and digital contract generation — helping students access funds quickly while maintaining trust and accountability.

Key Features

  • Track your loan status
  • Secure login and identity verification
  • AI-based risk score to protect lenders
  • Easy-to-read loan contracts and receipts

How it Works

  • Register and verify your student identity.
  • Browse loan requests or create a loan offer.
  • Agree on terms and sign digital contracts.
  • Track loan repayment and receive notifications.
